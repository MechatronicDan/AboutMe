3D Art

Welcome to my 3D Art page!

Ever since diving into game development, I was introduced to the world of 3D art. I was fascinated by every aspect of it, from modeling, texturing, animation, sculpting, and rendering. I use blender for all of my projects, as it is free and open source. The community is also very helpful and supportive. My projects vary from simple models to more complex scenes. Most of my art is inspired by games I have played or movies I have watched.

Projects Description
Scare Floor I recreated the Scare Floor from Monsters Inc. to a high level of accuracy. This project took around a month to complete and I learned a lot about modeling, texturing, animation, and geometry nodes.
Chloe's Bedroom This was a duo project where I collaborated with a friend to recreate Chloe's bedroom from Life is Strange.
Psyconauts Casino This was another duo project where we recreated the casino level from Psyconauts 2. We had to come up with very interesting solutions to create this scene to maintain accuracy. We also made the main menu and loading screen for the game as well.
Jinx's Pistol This was a solo project where I recreated Jinx's pistol from League of Legends. I learned a lot about hard surface modeling and texturing.
Up House This was a personal project where I recreated the Up house from the movie Up. I used geometry nodes to create the balloons, expanding my knowledge of procedural simulation.
